FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the free trial work?

You get full Pro access for 7 days. Build and export as many signatures as you like. If it's not for you, cancel in one click before the trial ends — simple and honest, no runaround.

Will the animation show in Gmail and Outlook?

Yes. Because we deliver an email-safe animated GIF, the motion plays in Gmail, Apple Mail, and most Outlook versions. A few older clients show the first frame — which we design to look great on its own.

What do I get on Pro for $8/month?

Your own logo in the signature, custom brand accent colors, every reveal direction and animation style, and removal of the "Made with" badge so it's fully your brand.

Can I set up my whole team?

Absolutely. Teams get a central dashboard, bulk generation, brand lock, and directory sync so everyone's signature stays on-brand. Reach out and we'll get you set up.

Do I need any design or technical skills?

None. Fill in your details, pick a template, and copy your signature into your email client. The whole thing takes about three minutes.
